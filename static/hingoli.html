Hingoli Tourism Guide

Explore the Historical and Spiritual Essence of Hingoli

Overview Things to Do When to Visit Precautions

Geographical Regions

  • Satpuda Ranges: Scenic hills and green landscapes
  • Godavari Basin: Fertile plains and river resources
  • Deccan Plateau: Flatlands with black soil suitable for farming.

Water Resources

Key features include Purna River and dams like Isapur, Kayadhu, and Siddheshwar, supporting irrigation and drinking water needs.

Urban Development

Growing infrastructure with road improvements, new markets, and better connectivity through rail and highways.

Cultural Experiences

  • Aundha Nagnath Temple:One of the 12 Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Mallinath Digambar Jain Temple: A major Jain pilgrimage site
  • Sant Namdev Sansthan: A sacred site honoring the revered saint Namdev.

Outdoor Adventures

  • Trekking at Aundha Hill:A spiritual trek with scenic views.
  • Kayadhu River Banks: A peaceful spot for picnics and nature walks.
  • Sant Janabai Park:A relaxing garden space for families and visitors

Food & Entertainment

  • Street food: Try Jowar Bhakri with Thecha, Pithla, and Hingoli Kachori.
  • Nightlife in local hubs – Small cafes and eateries in Hingoli city.
  • Annual Yatras & Fairs – Celebrations like Aundha Nagnath Mahotsav with cultural programs, food, and entertainment.
