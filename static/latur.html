General Safety

  • Use registered transportation and avoid unlicensed vehicles.
  • Keep your documents and valuables secure, especially in crowded areas
  • Stay hydrated, especially during summer, as Latur experiences high temperatures

Health Precautions

  • Carry essential medications, as pharmacies may not always have specific prescriptions
  • Wear sunscreen to protect against heat and sun exposure

Cultural Etiquette

  • Dress modestly at temples
  • Ask for permission before taking photos of locals or religious sites.
